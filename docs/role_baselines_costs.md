Role Baselines (Cost 1–8)

This document snapshots the role baseline tables used for balance targets. Role profiles (`data/identity/primary_role_profiles/*.tres`) encode only raw combat stats. Derived values (shields over T, heals over T, ability damage cadence, sustained DPS, EHP over 24s) are enforced in tests, not authored into stats.

Notes
- Attack speed baseline in role profiles is unified at `0.7` across all roles.
- Crit chance in baselines matches the table where applicable (e.g., Marksman 0.20, Assassin 0.15); deterministic test math may still bypass crit RNG.
- “Abilities”, “Sust. DPS”, and “EHP(24s)” are test targets derived from kit + formulas.

See also
- Test scene: `tests/rga2/FormulaRunner.tscn`
- Fixtures: `tests/rga2/fixtures/constants.json`, `tests/rga2/fixtures/role_targets.json`
