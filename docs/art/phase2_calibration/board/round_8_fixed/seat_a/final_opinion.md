`CONTINUE WORK`

The cut contains several strong individual monster and weapon silhouettes, but it does not yet satisfy the locked conjunctive gates for face-readable survival psychology, roster-wide individuality, visible supernatural cost and gameplay identity, genuine silhouette exploration, native-size communication, or the required auditable review record. ## Strongest contrary point

The strongest case for accepting the cut is that it is not broadly amateur or incoherent. Korath, Veyra, Nyxa, Creep, Kett, Sable, and Malachor have memorable dominant shapes; the twelve masters share a dry, detailed, grounded gothic rendering language; most figures read as mature combatants; the horror units avoid cute mascot drift; Korath visibly carries ruined sacred burden; and the packet includes a master, silhouette sheet, face crop, and native-size derivative for every unit. Those strengths make this a credible calibration cut rather than a failed art batch. They do not overcome the locked all-of gates: the missing unaided psychology, humanoid convergence, weak small-scale cost and trait communication, non-distinct silhouette explorations, contaminated comparisons, and absent executable review records remain material.
