# Veyra generation brief

Preserve a pale alien core and purple-blue identity accent while replacing humanoid glamour with a possessive architectural organism.

## Rough silhouettes

Exactly three distinct full-body black silhouettes on warm gray paper. A: tall narrow folding gate-organism. B: low six-limbed bastion. C: forward-lunging C-shaped living clamp on two braced legs, with one open sideways jaw gap and lateral closing plates around a prey void. No shield, weapon, wings, crystal crown, egg, passive cocoon, or readable text. Selected C.

## Definitive master

Full-body premium dark tactical-fantasy concept art of Veyra, a sexless alien shell-organism and possessive Tank that treats allies as contents. Selected C: an enormous dry chalky forward-lunging C-shaped living clamp on two braced legs. One side-facing jaw gap stays open around chosen prey while asymmetrical lateral plates, body lean, leg drive, and the off-center plum sensory slit all converge on that same prey vector. Aegis is nested enclosure; Bulwark is square locking architecture; cost is dead sensory slits, newly fused seams, and exposed tendon. At 96px it must read as an active predatory clamp before egg, armadillo, or passive cocoon. No external prop, human face, carried shield, robot coding, crystal glamour, spikes, gloss, VFX, or text.
