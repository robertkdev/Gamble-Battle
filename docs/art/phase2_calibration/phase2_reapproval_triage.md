## Non-negotiable carryovers

- No child, teen, elderly, or frail ordinary-humanoid fighter lane.
- Non-horrific women must read as hot adult women with lean, toned, feminine builds; no heavy-powerful female lane.
- Armor is never mandatory. It appears only when the unit's role, culture, body, and survival method justify it.
- Psychology must be specific and readable from the face; pose must reinforce the same survival strategy.
- Deliberately horrific characters may break ordinary attractiveness and age expectations, but the horror must be intentional, adult, threatening, and individually authored.
- One dominant prop or anatomical idea, one master concept, and only same-master face/96 px derivatives.
