# Laith — Redesign Candidate

Status: live character identity preserved; final gameplay redesign deferred.

Laith is an elegant human woman bound to a spine-mounted ledger. When she casts, the ledger drives a violent, pressurized expulsion of messy tattoo-black ink. The act is painful. The ink permanently stains her, and every use erodes her memories. Her silhouette, expression, and effects should communicate bodily cost and lost selfhood rather than wealth, bookkeeping power, or a cash pun.
